CBT END - TO - END

by Examic / in General / on November 1, 2019

examic takes over the complete management and conduct of the examination on your behalf. As a CBT end to end solutions provider, we handle all stages of the examination in a secure and efficient manner.
Our system can be configured to fit any specific requirements. Below are some of the primary activities undertaken.

Computer-Based Testing (CBT) end-to-end solutions for digital assessment portals offer comprehensive management of the entire exam lifecycle, from registration to results. Candidates register online through an intuitive portal, providing necessary details and selecting exam dates and centers based on availability. The system supports digital study materials and practice tests, with automated emails and notifications keeping candidates informed. Exam creation tools utilize a robust question bank, allowing for the design of exams with randomized questions and specific time limits. During exams, AI-based proctoring tools monitor candidates via webcams to ensure security and detect suspicious activities. Post-exam, the system facilitates automated grading for certain question types, providing instant feedback and detailed reports. This end-to-end solution enhances efficiency, accuracy, and security in the digital assessment process.
