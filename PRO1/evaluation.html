DIGITAL EVALUATION ON SCREEN MARKETING

by Examic / in Scenic / on May 24, 2019

Bring paper-based answer scripts online and eliminate logistical complexities with our on-screen marking solution.

Digital evaluation on-screen marking leverages technology to assess and grade student work electronically, offering significant advantages over traditional paper-based methods. Students submit their assignments, exams, or projects electronically via a learning management system (LMS) or a dedicated evaluation platform. Educators then use specialized software to view, annotate, and grade these digital submissions, with tools that support highlighting, commenting, and rubric-based scoring. For certain question types, such as multiple-choice or short answers, the software can automatically grade responses and provide instant feedback. This method enhances accuracy, streamlines the grading process, and allows for more detailed feedback to be given to students, ultimately improving the overall evaluation experience.
